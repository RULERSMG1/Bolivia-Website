A little bit about Bolivia

Bolivia, also known as the heart of South America is a small country located in South America.
it is surrounded by Argentina, Brazil, Paraguay, and Peru. Bolivia is slightly smaller than the state of Montana.
Although Bolivia is known to be one of the most poorest country's in South America, they are the country that exports products the most in all of South America.
It is also one of the most Beautiful country's to visit because of its vast heritage and different cultures across each state in the country.

What makes up Bolivia.

Bolivia is made out of nine departments.

  • Cochabamba
  • La Paz
  • Santa Cruz
  • Potosi
  • Chuquisaca
  • Beni
  • Pando
  • Tarija
  • Oruro
