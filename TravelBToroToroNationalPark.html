Toro Toro National Park is located betweek the departments of Potosi and Cochabamba. Toro Toro is a very great tourist location to visit. the park has vast canyons, caves, and a small archeological town that once belonged to the Quechua cultured people. In the caves of Toro Toro, you will be able to find over 2,500 dinosaur foot prints and fossils from the cretaceous period which was 120 million years ago. Toro Toro also has beautiful valleys to walk through and houses the endangered red-fonted rainbow macaw. Experts will be there to guide the tourists through the caves as it is a very big area to cover.
