Climate

The Climate in Bolivia is beautiful. It is warm all year round in most of the departments.
It is said to be the country of eternal spring. Its always a little chilly in the morning and night but nice and hot during the day.

Safety

Bolivia is a very safe place to visit. people are very friendly and are willing to help out when you have questions about where to go. Although Bolivia is a safe country, like any other country there is always some caution to have when traveling. pickpocketting and political protesting is very common so always pay attention to your belongings and never travel alone.
